We're students at IIT Gandhinagar, working on the Networks (CS 331) project. We have rigorously studied the CIF page, taking inspiration from the Tinkerer's Lab website at IITGN as well as other inventory management systems to develop this website. Our goal was to create a comprehensive inventory management solution specifically designed for the Central Instrumentation Facility. We would like to thank Prof. Sameer Kulkarni and the Teaching Assistants for their guidance and support throughout this project's development.

We were genuinely interested in learning how a website for inventory management would work in practice, and decided to build our own solution from scratch. This project allowed us to apply our knowledge of database systems, web development, and network programming to create a practical application. The hands-on experience gained through developing this system has deepened our understanding of real-world software development challenges and solutions. We hope this project demonstrates our commitment to creating useful, user-friendly tools that solve practical problems.
